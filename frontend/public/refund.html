Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: January 2025  |  BanyanVision

Return Window

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags intact.

Items Eligible for Return

  • Wrong item delivered
  • Damaged or defective product
  • Size/colour significantly different from the product page
  • Missing items from the order

Items NOT Eligible for Return

  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Customised or made-to-order items
  • Sale items marked as Final Sale

How to Initiate a Return

  • Email admin@banyanvision.com with your Order ID and reason for return
  • Attach photos of the item showing the issue
  • Our team will respond within 2 business days
  • We arrange free pickup from your doorstep

Refund Timeline

  • Original payment method: 5–7 business days after item received at our warehouse
  • UPI / Bank Transfer: 3–5 business days
  • Cash on Delivery orders: Refund via bank transfer — share account details with us

Exchange Policy

We offer free size exchanges within 7 days. Email us at admin@banyanvision.com with your Order ID and preferred size. Subject to stock availability.
