Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2025  |  BanyanVision

Delivery Timeframes

  • Standard Delivery: 3–5 business days — FREE on orders above ₹2,000
  • Express Delivery: 1–2 business days — ₹199
  • Remote areas: 5–8 business days

Delivery Charges

  • Orders above ₹2,000 — FREE Standard Delivery
  • Orders below ₹2,000 — ₹99 Standard Delivery
  • Express Delivery — ₹199 (available for most pin codes)
  • No delivery charges for exchanges

Shipping Partners

We ship via trusted partners including Delhivery, BlueDart, and India Post. You'll receive a tracking link via email once your order is dispatched.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Orders placed after 5 PM IST will be processed the next business day. No processing on Sundays and public holidays.

Tracking Your Order

Track your order anytime by logging in to your BanyanVision account and going to My Orders. You'll also receive email and WhatsApp updates at every stage.

Undelivered Orders

If a delivery attempt fails, our courier will try 2 more times. After 3 failed attempts, the package is returned to us and a full refund is initiated within 5–7 business days.
