Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025  |  BanyanVision

1. Introduction

Welcome to BanyanVision ("we", "our", "us"). We operate the website www.banyanvision.com and the BanyanVision mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

By using BanyanVision, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you shop on BanyanVision, we collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you register or checkout
  • Shipping address for order delivery
  • Payment information processed securely through Razorpay (we never store card details)
  • Order history and browsing preferences to improve your experience
  • Device information and app usage data (mobile app only) for performance monitoring

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To send order confirmation and shipping update emails/notifications
  • To provide customer support
  • To send promotional offers (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To improve our website and mobile application
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Razorpay — Payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Shipping partners (Delhivery, BlueDart, India Post) — Only name and address for delivery
  • MongoDB Atlas — Encrypted database storage
  • Cloudinary — Image hosting
  • Law enforcement — Only when required by law

5. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/SSL
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we cannot see your password
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay — we never store card numbers
  • Access tokens are stored securely using device-level encryption (mobile app)

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in across sessions
  • Remember your shopping cart
  • Analyse website performance (no advertising cookies)

You can clear cookies via your browser settings at any time. The mobile app uses secure device storage instead of cookies.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Order records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law. You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

BanyanVision is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Your Rights (India DPDP Act 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erase your account and associated personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Grievance redressal within 48 hours

To exercise any right, email us at: admin@banyanvision.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or via a prominent notice on our website/app. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Business: BanyanVision
  • Email: admin@banyanvision.com
  • Website: www.banyanvision.com
